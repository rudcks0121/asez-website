## The Zero Plastic 2040 framework

The campaign operates on three fronts:

- **Campus action** — single-use plastic bans, reusable infrastructure,
  measurable plastic-collection drives at participating universities
- **Public awareness** — Green Concerts, school programs, and youth
  forums that turn information into behavior change
- **Policy engagement** — youth delegations at the UN
  Intergovernmental Negotiating Committee meetings shaping the
  international plastic treaty

## Recent milestones

- **172,000 single-use cups avoided** in a single week-long reusable
  cup campaign across 50+ campuses
- **17 university chapters in India** pledged single-use plastic
  bans on their campuses by 2027
- **INC-5.2 policy paper** submitted with concrete recommendations
  on plastic production caps and Extended Producer Responsibility
