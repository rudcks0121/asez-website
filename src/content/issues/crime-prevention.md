## The Broken Windows approach

ASEZ's crime prevention work is built on a simple idea: visible signs
of neglect — broken windows, graffiti, abandoned spaces — signal that
no one is watching, which invites more serious crime. Visible care
reverses that signal.

## What the work looks like

- **Mural and wall restoration** — chapters partner with local
  municipalities to repair and beautify neglected public spaces
- **Community walks** — joint patrols with residents and local
  authorities, surveying conditions and surfacing what needs fixing
- **Youth-led safety forums** — bringing students, police, and
  community elders to one table to redesign public space

## Measurable outcomes

Partner cities consistently report lower vandalism and improved
resident sentiment in the six months following an ASEZ
Reduce-Crime-Together intervention.
