## The work we don't always headline

Community service is the foundation under everything else ASEZ does.
While our climate and crime-prevention programs get the headlines,
local chapters spend most of their hours on quiet, consistent work:

- **Elderly care** — meal deliveries, companionship visits,
  technology support for senior citizens
- **School partnerships** — tutoring, after-school programs,
  mentoring for underserved students
- **Local NGO support** — providing volunteer labor to small
  organizations that lack capacity

## Why this matters as much as the rest

A student who shows up every Saturday at the same community center
learns more about real change than any climate forum can teach. And
the communities we serve get something institutions struggle to
deliver: presence, consistency, and care.
