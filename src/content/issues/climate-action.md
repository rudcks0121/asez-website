## What we're working on

ASEZ runs three flagship climate programs in parallel:

- **Blue Carbon Movement** — restoring mangroves, seagrass beds, and tidal
  marshes across seven coastal countries. These ecosystems store carbon
  at rates many times higher than terrestrial forests.
- **Global Tree Planting** — coordinated planting actions across Africa,
  Asia, Latin America, and the Pacific. The 2026 campaign targets
  1 million trees in regions affected by desertification.
- **Climate Policy Engagement** — youth-led delegations to UN climate
  negotiations, presenting evidence-based recommendations on forests,
  oceans, and energy transition.

## Why student-led works

Climate action led by under-30 volunteers consistently outperforms older
operational models on cost-per-hectare and 5-year survival rates. We
move fast, we listen to local communities, and we don't wait for
institutional permission.
