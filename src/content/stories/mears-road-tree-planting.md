The Mears Road site in Pretoria became the launchpad for the broader
2026 Restore the Earth campaign. ASEZ volunteers planted 20 trees
across the site in a single afternoon — a small but symbolic milestone
that proved the operational playbook before scaling.

The first phase tested the full chain of activity that would later be
repeated at nine sites in March:

- Site selection and soil readiness check
- Coordination with municipal partners for permits
- Volunteer mobilization and safety briefing
- Planting, mulching, and watering protocols
- Documentation for the campaign log

Each step is now codified so the next chapter — Kenya, in May — can
launch without re-inventing the process.
