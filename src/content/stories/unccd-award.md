The United Nations Convention to Combat Desertification (UNCCD)
presented ASEZ with the Executive Secretary's Award in recognition
of our sustained contribution to global tree planting and land
restoration efforts.

The award, presented during the COP16 session in Riyadh, cited:

- ASEZ's documented planting of over 1 million trees across 53 countries
  in the past five years
- Active involvement in restoration of degraded lands in Africa,
  Latin America, and Central Asia
- Youth mobilization as a model that other organizations are
  beginning to replicate

UNCCD Executive Secretary recognized ASEZ's "consistent ability to
turn principles into ground-level action at a scale most institutions
struggle to match."

The award joins ASEZ's recognition from the U.S. presidency, the
Green Apple Awards, and the Green World Awards.
