ASEZ representatives met with the President of the Democratic Republic
of Timor-Leste to formalize a national-level Support Agreement, marking
one of the first such bilateral arrangements between ASEZ and a head of
state.

The agreement establishes:

1. A national framework for new ASEZ campus chapters at Timor-Leste's
   public universities
2. Cooperation on coastal restoration and Blue Carbon Movement projects
   along the Timor Sea
3. Joint youth-led research on local climate resilience

The signing ceremony took place in Dili and was attended by ministry
officials, ASEZ regional leadership, and student representatives from
prospective chapter campuses.

Timor-Leste joins a growing list of countries — including South Korea,
the Philippines, and Kenya — with formal national partnerships
supporting ASEZ activity.
