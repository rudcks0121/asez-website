The ASEZ Green Concert returned to Hyderabad with one message:
**plastic pollution is solvable in our lifetime, if we act now.**

Thousands of university students, civic partners, and local government
representatives joined the day-long event — combining performances,
exhibitions, and the formal launch of the next phase of our
**Zero Plastic 2040** campaign.

Highlights from the day:

- A pledge signed by 17 university chapters across India committing
  to single-use plastic bans on their campuses by 2027
- A public exhibition of campus-led plastic-collection data — 12,000+
  kilograms diverted from waterways in the past year
- Workshops on negotiating with municipal vendors to switch to
  refillable systems

The Hyderabad concert is the third in a series. Earlier events ran
in Manila and Lagos. Bogotá is next, in 2026.
